### Summary
On [Date], a ransomware incident was detected on the organization's network, resulting in potential data exfiltration and disruption of business operations. The incident response team was activated, and containment measures were taken to prevent further spread.

### Root Cause
The most likely attack path and entry point is through a phishing campaign targeting employees, resulting in successful login attempts from an unknown IP address. The attacker then used this access to create ransom demands on the file server and encrypt sensitive data.

### Recommended Next Actions

1. **Reconstruct attacker's domain**: Investigate the IP address used for the initial login attempts to determine if it was part of a malicious campaign.
2. **Verify backups**: Confirm that the protected backups are indeed immutable and have not been tampered with.
3. **Conduct post-incident review**: Schedule a review within 5 business days to document timeline, root cause, and control gaps.
4. **Submit cyber insurance claim**: File required regulatory and contractual breach notifications and submit a cyber insurance claim package to the insurer.
5. **Implement remediation actions**: Update detection content, update network segmentation rules, and implement new security measures to prevent similar incidents in the future.
